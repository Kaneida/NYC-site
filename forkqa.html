New York Coin

Fork Q&A

1. I’m new to NewYorkCoin, how long has this been in development?
According to the original bitcointalk thread, NewYorkCoin was created in March of 2014. However according to the original Github, no major coding updates to keep up with the times has happened until a revived interest occurred between the end of 2017 and beginning of 2018. A few developers from the community decided to volunteer their time and talents towards revising the code since then in this Github: https://github.com/NewYorkCoin-NYC.
2. Why is the roadmap changing near the end of the 2nd quarter?
Due to current news involving attacks on two well known coins, the developers reviewed their priorities and realized that security must be number one so that such attacks do not happen to NewYorkCoin.
3. What will the developers do to secure NewYorkCoin against attacks?
The intention of the security enhancements involve: Adding Digishield (instead of the current Kimoto Gravity Well) which reacts better to sudden hashrate increase/decrease , Add merged mining (AuxPow) to have pools like litecoinpool.org automatically also mine NYC while they mine LTC and Advanced Checkpointing (as in Feathercoin). During the "revamp ", block reward reduction will be added as well.
4. Wouldn’t a reward reduction be a soft fork? Or due to the digishield and merged mining it’s a hard one now?
Block reward reduction is a soft fork The others are not. This move will also stabilize the network and prevent large pools from hopping on and off.
5. How would this major change to the roadmap help NewYorkCoin increase its price and potential to be listed on exchanges?
These changes would not only resolve the inflation issue of NYCoin (block reward reduction) but also show the exchange staff that initially review $NYC’s coding that NewYorkCoin is a secure coin, safe from attacks that would affect the exchange. Most exchanges do not like constant roadmap deviations but specific updates that affect security and have a mutual benefit are taken into consideration. Having a more secure and stable network will also assure potential investors that their investment will not come under constant attack.
